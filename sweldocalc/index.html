Sweldo Calc

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026
Developer: Raiko Software Development Services
The short version Sweldo Calc works fully offline. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data. All computations happen on your device and never leave it.

1. Information We Collect

Sweldo Calc does not collect any personally identifiable information. The app operates entirely offline and does not require an account, login, or any personal details to function.

All salary figures, tax computations, and financial data you enter into the app exist only on your device and are never transmitted to any server.

2. Data Storage

Any data stored by the app (such as computation history for Pro users) is saved locally on your device using on-device storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app

3. In-App Purchases

Sweldo Calc offers optional in-app purchases (Pro — Employee and Pro — Freelancer). All purchase transactions are processed entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information, billing details, or purchase history beyond what the platform provides to confirm a successful transaction.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may display advertisements via Google AdMob on the free tier. AdMob may collect certain device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. Pro users who have disabled ads are not subject to AdMob data collection.

In-app purchases are handled by Apple (iOS) and Google (Android), subject to their respective privacy policies.

5. Analytics

We do not use any analytics, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs in Sweldo Calc. We do not know how many times you use the app, which features you use, or any other behavioral data.

6. Children's Privacy

Sweldo Calc is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is intended for working adults and professionals.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete on our end. If you want to remove all locally stored data, simply uninstall the app.
